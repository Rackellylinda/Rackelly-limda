Penalty Kick Game

Trivia Questions

Question 1: What is a penalty kick?
Answer: A penalty kick is awarded for a foul inside the penalty area.

Question 2: How far is the penalty spot from the goal?
Answer: The penalty spot is 12 yards (11 meters) from the goal line.

Question 3: Who was the first player to score a penalty kick in football history?
Answer: The first recorded penalty kick was taken by a player named Donald Mackinlay in 1891.
